🌟 Types of Harassment 🌟

✨ You are brave for being here. This is a safe space where your voice matters and your experiences are valid. Together, we can create a world where everyone feels safe and respected. 💜

🏢Workplace Harassment

Inappropriate behavior, discrimination, or abuse of power in professional settings.

Examples include:

  • Unwanted sexual advances from colleagues/bosses
  • Discrimination based on gender
  • Inappropriate comments about appearance
  • Creating hostile work environment
  • Salary discrimination

🚶‍♀️Street Harassment

Unwanted attention, comments, or behavior in public spaces.

Examples include:

  • Catcalling and vulgar comments
  • Following or stalking in public
  • Inappropriate touching on public transport
  • Taking photos without consent
  • Blocking path or cornering

💻Cyber Harassment

Online abuse, threats, or harassment through digital platforms.

Examples include:

  • Cyberbullying and online threats
  • Sharing intimate images without consent
  • Online stalking and monitoring
  • Fake profiles and impersonation
  • Harassment on social media

🏠Domestic Violence

Physical, emotional, or financial abuse within household or relationships.

Examples include:

  • Physical violence and threats
  • Emotional and psychological abuse
  • Financial control and isolation
  • Controlling behavior and monitoring
  • Threats to family members

⚠️Sexual Harassment

Unwanted sexual behavior, advances, or assault in any setting.

Examples include:

  • Unwanted touching or groping
  • Sexual assault or rape
  • Pressuring for sexual favors
  • Sharing or requesting intimate content
  • Making sexual jokes or comments

🎓Academic Harassment

Harassment in educational institutions by peers, teachers, or staff.

Examples include:

  • Sexual harassment by teachers/professors
  • Bullying by classmates
  • Discrimination in grades or opportunities
  • Inappropriate behavior during mentoring
  • Ragging or hazing incidents
